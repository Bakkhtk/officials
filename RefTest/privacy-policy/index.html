RefTest - Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 25, 2025

What RefTest is

RefTest is a watch application that provides interval timing and related on-device features for referees. The app runs fully on your watch and does not require an internet connection to function.

No data collection

RefTest does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. We do not use analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services that send data off-device. We do not receive or access your usage, logs, or personal information.

Local data stored on your device

Any settings, saved intervals, or preferences used by RefTest are stored locally on your watch. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party. If you uninstall the app or clear app data on your device, those local items will be removed according to your device’s normal behavior.

Third-party content & libraries

The running app does not fetch or load third-party content at runtime. Any libraries or assets that are included in the app are bundled at build time. The app has no external dependencies that collect or send user data while it runs.

Support & contact

If you have questions about this policy or need technical support, contact the development team at:

Email: dev@officialstech.com

Because RefTest does not collect user data, we generally cannot retrieve or delete user-specific information from our side. If you need help removing local data from your device, tell us your device model and we will advise how to clear app data.

Children

RefTest is not intended to collect information from children. As the app does not collect or transmit personal data, no special protections are required in practice.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy in a way that affects how user information is handled, we will publish an updated policy with a new effective date. For the latest version, contact dev@officialstech.com.

Legal notes

We aim to be transparent and minimal with user information. This policy is provided for clarity and does not create contractual rights. If you believe the app is behaving in a way that violates this policy, contact us with details at dev@officialstech.com.
